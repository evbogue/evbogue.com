<p>Try explaining to someone who hasn&#8217;t used Twitter that we&#8217;re becoming a race of cyborgs, they&#8217;ll look at you like you just teleported in from Gallifrey.</p>
<p>The reality is that we did just teleport in. We&#8217;re becoming a new form of convergent homo sapien: time travelers with wormholes in our pockets&#8230; and it&#8217;s only a short time before those wormholes move from our pockets to somewhere far more useful.</p>
<p>I want you to watch this, because I hope it can make what is happening to you make sense. <a href="http://twitter.com/#!/caseorganic">Amber Case</a> is one of the must beautiful fraking brilliant geniuses on the planet at this very moment. I can&#8217;t explain to you in words how important this information is to you, me, and the world we&#8217;re working towards.</p>

<p>We are all becoming cyborgs. You&#8217;re either one of us, or you will be soon. You can try to fight it, deny it, but it won&#8217;t change the fact that this is happening.</p>
<p>You&#8217;re a part of this, and the change is accelerating with the exponential nature of technology.</p>
<p>Yesterday I <a href="http://www.twitter.com/evbogue">Twittered</a>: &#8220;You do not want to be a control group in exponential evolution. It&#8217;s just a really bad idea. We have to upgrade or yikes.&#8221; This much is true.</p>
<p>Some of you are being left behind, lost in the noise. A great deal of the build up in/of frustration, anger, jealousy that you&#8217;re seeing in your world is directly related to this change.</p>
<p>One of my foremost worries right now: what if the divide becomes too vast? We&#8217;re going to be exploring this in more detail here, on <a href="http://www.twitter.com/evbogue">Twitter</a>, and in the new e-book we&#8217;re working on.</p>
<p>All cyborgs will need to learn to breathe &#8211;this is our practice.</p>
<p>Let&#8217;s all embrace our own humanity, in the things we&#8217;ve created.
