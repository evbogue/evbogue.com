<p><a href="/assets/posts/the-dreams-of-a-minimalist-army-img1.jpg"><img class="alignleft" src="/assets/posts/the-dreams-of-a-minimalist-army-img1.jpg" alt="" width="263" height="350"/></a>The  last few weeks have been quite a whirlwind of activity. I&#8217;ve released an  e-book, I&#8217;ve had the honor of being featured on a number of amazing blogs, such as my <a href="http://zenhabits.net/2010/02/creative-flow/">article on creative flow at Zen  Habits</a> recently.</p>
<p>I love reading the links that people send to me offering their opinions on what I&#8217;ve created.</p>
<p>The  experience has been amazing. I&#8217;ve received many emails asking me how I  did it. I&#8217;m not entirely sure, but I&#8217;d love to try and figure it out.</p>
<p>I like to think that it&#8217;s just writing quality work every single day. Creating value, in order to help you, is all that I think about.</p>
<p>I started  out with a small idea: what if I quit my job, gave up everything but  the essential, got on an airplane, and went for a trip. I came back with  an inspiration that&#8217;s sweeping the imagination of an amazing group of  people.</p>
<p>It&#8217;s becoming so abundantly  clear that this isn&#8217;t about me at all, it&#8217;s all about you.</p>
<p><strong>The dreams of a minimalist army.</strong></p>
<p>The truth is that I&#8217;m just one of the many amazing people who are leading this revolution &#8212; for more of the talented folks who are blogging about being minimalist, see my blogroll.</p>
<p>I&#8217;ve also compiled a list of some of the newer bloggers below. Check them out! If I missed you, leave a comment and I&#8217;ll add you to the list.</p>
<p><strong>Do you want to join the minimalist army?</strong></p>
<p>It&#8217;s not easy to succeed on the Internet (or in anything) alone. I need your help to spread the idea of being minimalist to as many people as possible.</p>

<p>You can start your own minimalist blog, like the list of people below.</p>
<p>You can help support my writing, and also earn some money yourself by signing up for the <a href="http://artofbeingminimalist.com/affiliates.html"><em>Art of Being Minimalist</em> affiliate program</a>.</p>
<p>&#8211;</p>
<p>You are the inspiration for the freedom of  the life I&#8217;m living. You are  the reason that I keep pushing for this change.</p>
<p>I can see this in every thank you letter I  receive. I can see this in every helpful comment, and every amazing  retweet of a post. We all wanted this change. We all needed to know that  it was possible.</p>
<p>It is possible.</p>
