<p>This e-book isn&#8217;t for everyone. If you&#8217;re content to sit at home, watch TV, and embrace the status-quo&#8230; well, you won&#8217;t find much information that helps you here.</p>
<p><em>How to Create a Movement</em> is for people who want to help people, support themselves through their art, and change the world.</p>
<p><strong>Why I wrote <em>How to Create a Movement</em>.</strong></p>
<p>I continue to get emails from smart people who are seeking change, who want to learn my &#8217;secrets&#8217; to making money online. There are no secrets, but I hope this can help.</p>
<p>I believe that the easiest way to find success online is through creating or joining a movement. A movement is anything that seeks to change the world in a small way. There are a number of mediums you can use to create one. Some create a movement through art, others through music, speaking.</p>
<p>I choose to create a movement through words and ideas.</p>
<p>I hope this e-book can help you start your own movements. I hope that it helps you find greater success in your life. Most of all, I hope it helps you change the lives of others.</p>
