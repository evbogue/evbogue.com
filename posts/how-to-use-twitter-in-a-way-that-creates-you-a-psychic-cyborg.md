<p>1. Follow less than 150 people. Your human brain can&#8217;t breathe if you exceed that limit. Less is always more. Start large, but work your way down, not up.</p>
<p>2. It&#8217;s a river, you can&#8217;t drink it all.</p>
<p>3. Who cares about DMs, public @.</p>
<p>4. Who cares who follows you.</p>
<p>5. Picture of your Face, because you&#8217;re beautiful and human.</p>
<p>6. Unfollow anyone who you no(know)-longer recognize (people who are noise).</p>
<p>7. Send only positive energy. Information is a form of energy.</p>
<p>8. Follow the people I follow and you&#8217;ll turn into us.</p>
<p>9. For every hour of Twitter = one hour of yoga. You can breathe and use Twitter at the same time. Breathing is yoga = pranayama. Asana will teach you to breathe and Tweet.</p>
<p>10. 1 hour of Internet is currently 40 in RL. It&#8217;s headed toward escape velocity.</p>
<p>Good, now you&#8217;re on your way to becoming a #psychictechnoninja. This is what facilitates #ibc. Intuitive Backdoor Channel. Also #convergence.</p>
<p>Chances are you have no idea what I&#8217;m talking about, but you will when you do the above for x-days. Leap of faith.</p>
