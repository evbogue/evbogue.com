<p><strong>What is Minimalist Workday?</strong></p>
<p><em><span style="font-style: normal;">I&#8217;ve been tossing around the idea that our average workday is too long for awhile now. </span></em></p>
<p><em><span style="font-style: normal;">Over the past year I&#8217;ve managed to build a business working an average of 2 hours a week. I&#8217;m not saying this to brag, but simply to let you know that it&#8217;s possible. </span></em></p>
<p><em><span style="font-style: normal;">When you make slight behavioral modifications like turning off e-mail and focusing on what is important for your business, it&#8217;s amazing what you can accomplish.</span></em></p>
<p><em><span style="font-style: normal;">Minimalist Workday outlines 50 strategies that I use to keep my workday at a comfortable 2 hours a day. This way I can dedicate more time to research, travel, self-improvement, cooking good food, yoga, and helping others.</span></em></p>
<p><em><span style="font-style: normal;">This started as a blog post, but spiraled out of control. The e-book is around 5,000 words, over the course of 30 pages. I hope that it helps you.</span></em></p>
<p><strong>Why is Minimalist Workday free?</strong></p>
<p>To be honest, I probably could have charged for this information, but then it wouldn&#8217;t help as many people. I&#8217;ve already doubled my income over the last year, and while more money is always better, some ideas need to be free to have the largest impact.</p>
<p><strong>Minimalist Workday isn&#8217;t for everyone.</strong></p>
<p>To be honest, I wrote this specifically with self-employed professionals who are running simple one-person businesses in mind. It really is a free addition to <em><a href="http://www.minimalistbusiness.com/">Minimalist Business</a></em>.</p>
<p>That being said, with a little mental tweaking, I imagine you can apply this knowledge to a 9-5 in order to free up more time for launching extra-income earning side-projects or get yourself promoted. If you&#8217;re part of a ROWE company, even better.</p>
