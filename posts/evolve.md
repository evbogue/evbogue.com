#### What is Evolving Your Digital Work?

Evolving Your Digital Work is a Letter from me to you, about the raw, unedited, important stuff that isn't ready for the public web yet. I've been writing it since December, 2010. When I write it, I ask myself: what's truly alive in you, Everett? Then I write from a place of openness (knowing that it's going out to a group of less than three hundred people.)

There's no archive, so the work moves through time with me. I untether from pieces the moment after they've been published. You're welcome to save them, if they resonate with you.

It's my intention to share what the Letter's about, not sell it to you. I can't explain *exactly* what it's about, because that changes through time. I do know that I write it at least once a week. I do know that the work trends towards lengthier, deeper, and more intimate posts than what I publish to the blog. The blog is an invitation to go deeper, this letter is the depth.

Evolving Your Digital Work isn't for everyone. It's for a small group of people who are deeply invested in the work that I'm doing. I give you a glimpse into the harder more intimate stuff, in exchange for your help supporting my work. If you're new to my work, don't subscribe right away. You're welcome to read through the blog, and sign up for free updates first.

#### About Ev

Ev Bogue's work in online publishing has been featured on The CBS Evening News with Katie Couric, The Guardian UK, and The Huffington Post. Ev has spoken about publishing online at Stanford University. Ev has worked in blogging at New York Magazine and Gawker Media. [Learn More](/posts/about).
