The primary way my writing travels is by word of mouth.

There are a couple of ways this happens, these days.

1. Forward this to a friend. If you receive the free daily letters, you can always forward the emails to a friend using your email client.

2. Link on your blog. Do you blog? Links are what makes the Internet go around. In the olden days, links were all we had. Things change, but links stay the same. They're the most permanent connection to make.

3. Experiment. Have you come up with an interesting way to spread the word? Drop me an email.

I'm also interested to hear: How does word spread about your work?
