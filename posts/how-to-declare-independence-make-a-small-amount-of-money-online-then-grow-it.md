<p><a href="/assets/posts/how-to-declare-independence-make-a-small-amount-of-money-online-then-grow-it-img1.jpg"><img class="alignleft" src="/assets/posts/how-to-declare-independence-make-a-small-amount-of-money-online-then-grow-it-img1.jpg" alt="" width="262" height="350"/></a>Without a doubt, every single person I&#8217;ve met who has a command of the ability to make their own money is more charismatic.</p>
<p>The reason for this is simple: if you have mastery of the ability to provide value in order to generate income it boosts your self-confidence. Then you can declare independence from the system of wage slavery.</p>
<p>You&#8217;re no longer scared of losing your job, because you know if you do it&#8217;ll only be a matter of time before you build up your own empire.</p>
<p><strong>How to declare independence.</strong></p>
<p>Most of the people who I meet have jobs that they&#8217;re terrified to lose. This makes me sad, especially now that I&#8217;m on the other side &#8212; I&#8217;d never let anyone pay me to work for them even if they begged me!</p>
<p>Why would you sit at a desk all day waiting to die when you have an opportunity to make a small amount of money online and then grow it?</p>
<p>In fact, I&#8217;d like to argue that making a small amount of money online is incredibly easy. Making $4.95 can&#8217;t be hard right? This article will show you how. Once you make $4.95, you can try for $10 in a day, then make your way towards $80 (which is all you really need anyway.)</p>
<p>Alright, blah blah money making this and that. You&#8217;ve heard this all before right?</p>
<p>Well no, because I intend to explain to you exactly how you can declare independence by earning an incredibly small amount of money with minimal effort.</p>
<p><strong>A history of making money online.</strong></p>
<p>You see, back when I was in Portland more than a year ago now, I had no idea how to make money online even though I&#8217;d worked online for four years before that.</p>
<p>I had this weird misconception &#8211;that I think a lot of people share,&#8211; that in order to develop income online I would have to write about Lady Gaga and place advertisements against the stories. People would click, I would make a little bit of money.</p>
<p>This is one way to make money online, the problem is that it&#8217;s very hard to do this. You see,<strong> no one clicks on ads</strong> anymore. I certainly don&#8217;t. Do you click on ads? Not if you&#8217;re smart, and if you&#8217;re reading this blog without sipping on a coke while stuck in traffic driving your SUV I know you are quite smart.</p>
<p>Advertising is a bad business model on the web for most businesses, because it takes a massive amount of readers to sustain your business off dumb people clicking on ads.</p>
<p>I<strong>f you aim for a dumb audience, you have to write a dumb blog</strong>. I certainly didn&#8217;t want to do that, and neither do you. I realize a certain percentage of my readers are absolute idiots, but I&#8217;m not writing this for them. I&#8217;m writing it for you smart people out there. Dumb people: this is your cue to unsubscribe. This blog isn&#8217;t for you.</p>
<p><strong>How I made my first $4.95 online, and you can too.</strong></p>
<p>Back to the future in Portland, I was sitting with a Stumptown coffee being incredibly stumped about how to make a living online, and it hit me:</p>
<ol>
<li>I&#8217;m writing a blog about minimalism.</li>
<li>One of my favorite books is about minimalism.</li>
<li>My favorite book has a 50% affiliate program.</li>
</ol>
<p>So, that day I signed up for a free account with <a href="http://www.e-junkie.com/?r=91858">e-junkie</a> and dropped a link to <em><a href="https://www.e-junkie.com/ecom/gb.php?ii=329069&amp;c=ib&amp;aff=91858&amp;cl=10747">A Simple Guide to a Minimalist Life</a></em> somewhere in a blog post.</p>
<p>I published something simple like this in my blog post:</p>
<p>&#8220;One of my huge inspirations for living a minimalist life came from reading Leo Babauta&#8217;s <em>A Simple Guide to a Minimalist Life</em>.&#8221;</p>
<p>Then I did some Yoga, burned some incense, and went to bed while the Portland rain splattered quietly on my windows.</p>
<p>The next morning I woke up, made breakfast, and then went out into the world with my laptop to grab a coffee. When I opened my email, to my surprise a little message was there:</p>
<p>&#8220;Sale &#8211; Leo Babauta- ID:XXXXXXXX The Simple Guide to a Minimalist Life&#8221;</p>
<p>I almost fell over backwards because for the first time in my life I&#8217;d paid for my coffee BEFORE I&#8217;d bought it!</p>
<p><strong>I&#8217;d made money in my sleep! Yay!</strong></p>
<p>Now, $4.95 might not seem like a lot of money, but at the time I was making zip, nada, nothing. Taking this small step really gave me a lot of confidence that this whole business model might actually work.</p>
<p>You only need to be making $80 a day to do $30,000 a year. Only one year after making this discovery, I&#8217;m doing far more than $80 a day. You can too, but you have to start at $4.95 first.</p>
<p>If you think about it, how much harder is it to make $80 after you&#8217;ve made $4.95? Actually, not much. The biggest leap is the first dollar. You don&#8217;t need to be making millions, just $80. It&#8217;s not as hard as you think it is.</p>
<p><strong>What is e-junkie?</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.e-junkie.com/?r=91858">E-junkie</a> is a pretty basic service that handles most of my business automation. It&#8217;s free for people who are affiliating for products, but only costs $5 a month if you&#8217;re selling a digital product. I use e-junkie for two purposes:</p>
<p>1. To sell other people&#8217;s products and get commission. I can&#8217;t make every product in the world myself, so luckily I can make a small profit off selling other people&#8217;s products. For example, last month I made nearly $1,000 in a day selling Corbett Barr&#8217;s <em><a href="https://www.e-junkie.com/ecom/gb.php?cl=79691&amp;c=ib&amp;aff=91858">Affiliate Marketing for Beginners</a></em>. Of course you all remember the day I made <a href="http://www.farbeyondthestars.com/2300-in-a-day-how-to-support-quality-work/">$2,300 selling one of the most important digital works thus far</a>.</p>
<p>2. To sell my own products. <a href="http://www.e-junkie.com/?r=91858">E-junkie</a> handles the distribution, payment, and affiliate payouts for my products. This means that I don&#8217;t have to e-mail my book to people every time they buy it, which is amazing. This means I can focus exclusively on writing, and not have to worry about the administration side of my business &#8212; because it&#8217;s all automated. Automation is covered extensively in <em><a href="http://minimalistbusiness.com/">Minimalist Business</a></em>.</p>
<p><strong>How not to be sketchy.</strong></p>
<p>One of the biggest take-aways from my ongoing conversations-over-many-beers with superstar marketing and <a href="http://www.thinktraffic.net/">website traffic generating expert Corbett Barr</a> has been that you CANNOT get away with being sketchy as a marketer online.</p>
<p>Big flashing red buttons, pyramid schemes, and selling people shitty products that don&#8217;t help them are out. Instead, affiliating for products that actually help people is the future.</p>
<p>I never recommend products that didn&#8217;t contribute to my life, you shouldn&#8217;t either.</p>
<p>Also, remember that most &#8212; probably 95% of your readers don&#8217;t necessarily need the product you&#8217;re recommending. So, don&#8217;t bang them over the head with it. Just say &#8220;hey, I read this e-book and it taught me about this. If you&#8217;re into this, maybe check it out.&#8221; Simple, but it actually works.</p>
<p>Big red &#8216;buy this NOW&#8217; buttons do not work. Instead, our sketchification alarm goes off and we bolt.</p>
<p>Also, after extensive testing, I really believe that putting pictures of e-books in your sidebar as recommendations doesn&#8217;t sell them either. Pictures in sidebars scream advertising to most people.</p>

<p><strong>Strategies for landing your first few dollars.</strong></p>
<p>Finally, here are the top two strategies that I use to sell products that matter on Far Beyond the Stars.</p>
<p>Pay attention! These strategies actually run counter to what Problogger or Copyblogger will tell you to do.</p>
<p>Why am I dissing these guys? Because I read them both for three years and never learned how to do what I&#8217;m teaching you to do here &#8212; instead they just kept sending me a fluffy post per day about nothing important and trying to sell me stuff I don&#8217;t need.</p>
<p>Plus, my blog is almost as big as Copyblogger now, and I&#8217;ve only been doing this for a year. I must be doing something right.</p>
<p>If you want to learn how to start a blog, read either <a href="http://www.viperchill.com/">Viperchill</a> or <a href="http://thinktraffic.net/">Think Traffic</a>. These sites actually tell you how to build a blog, instead of just sending you a fluffy post every day with information that isn&#8217;t helpful like the aforementioned larger blogs.</p>
<p><strong>I&#8217;ve tried a lot of strategies, these two get the best results.</strong></p>
<p><strong>1. Interviews with authors.</strong></p>
<p>My #1 strategy for selling work that matters is an interview with the author. Ask the author &#8220;how will this help my readers achieve their specific goal?&#8221; and other questions. Focus on the benefits of the product. Make sure to put an affiliate link to the product in the post in a few key points. Make it clear that if your audience buys this, they will support your blog and the author of the product.</p>
<p><strong>2. The quick mention in-post.</strong></p>
<p>Sometimes if I&#8217;m talking about a subject that&#8217;s similar to a product I&#8217;ve read, I&#8217;ll mention it casually. For example, if I&#8217;m writing about personal finance, I always say &#8220;hey, if you want to learn more about getting out of debt, one of the best books I ever read on the subject was Adam Baker&#8217;s <em><a href="https://www.e-junkie.com/ecom/gb.php?ii=629180&amp;c=ib&amp;aff=91858&amp;cl=80780">Unautomate Your Finances</a></em>.&#8221;</p>
<p>Believe it or not, the casual reference does WAY better than trying to do a review of a product in the traditional sense. A review assumes your entire audience might want the product, which isn&#8217;t the case. You&#8217;re making a whole bunch of people who don&#8217;t care read something they probably don&#8217;t want to read.</p>
<p>I don&#8217;t like reading reviews, do you? No, because you&#8217;re smart. Interviews and casual mentions work best &#8212; but obviously do your own testing to see what works best with your audience.</p>
<p><strong>Obviously making money online isn&#8217;t for everyone.</strong></p>
<p>Some of you are happy sitting all day at a desk working 40 hours a week. The point of this article is simple: learning how to make a small amount of money, even $4.95, can boost your self-confidence immensely and allow you to begin to start declaring independence.</p>
<p>If you know you could build a business making $80 a day on your own, you&#8217;ll be less afraid of taking risks with your life. This means if your trying to land a raise or a promotion, you won&#8217;t be worried about walking out the door if your boss hands you that 2% raise that you didn&#8217;t ask for.</p>
<p>Declaring independence is the key to developing the valuable charisma necessary to become a leader. A big part of that is knowing that you can go it alone if you need to.</p>
<p>This way if someone tells you to sit down and shut up, you can tell them to shove it.</p>
<p>You have the keys to becoming an independent freedom fighter now.</p>
<p>Now you can support yourself, pay your readers, and make a difference.</p>
