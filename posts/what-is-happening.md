<p><a href="/assets/posts/what-is-happening-img1.jpg"><img class="alignleft" src="/assets/posts/what-is-happening-img1.jpg" alt="" width="262" height="350"/></a>A lot of people don&#8217;t know what&#8217;s going on, so I thought I might try and convey it to you.</p>
<p>The Internet killed the Gatekeepers and made it possible to connect with anyone anywhere in the world.</p>
<p>This enabled like-minded individuals to speak to each other across all borders effortlessly, without censorship.</p>
<p>So, for the first time in the history of the human race, the people who actually get it &#8212; the ones who are awake,&#8211; are able to organize themselves to save the future of the human race.</p>
<p>Ideas don&#8217;t come from the individual, they come from the formation of a collective. I&#8217;m just funneling the ideas of my collective whole, and you are to pass them on. There are no boundaries for ideas.</p>
<p>If you want to see evidence of the above statement, check out <a href="http://www.ted.com/talks/chris_anderson_how_web_video_powers_global_innovation.html">Chris Anderson of TED&#8217;s latest talk</a>. Technology is accelerating innovation.</p>
<p>The good ideas spread, the bad ideas die.</p>
<p><strong>This feedback loop is accelerating toward escape velocity.</strong></p>
<p>In the near future&#8230;</p>
<p>We will end poverty (both the 5-bedroom 2-car TV-enslavement in the 1st world and the we-can&#8217;t-eat in the 3rd)</p>
<p>Will will destroy the corporations that led us to this place of advertising enslavement.</p>
<p>We will recognize that we&#8217;re in this together. We brought us to this place, and we will take us to the future.</p>
<p>We will find freedom and also harmony for all. Across all borders, ideals, and end all conflicts.</p>
<p>This is why minimalism is blowing up right now, because it is the truth, because it brings about freedom for all.</p>
<p>This has been tried before, but before we didn&#8217;t have the tools without boundaries.</p>
<p>They can&#8217;t stop us now.</p>
<p>Are you alive? Prove it.
