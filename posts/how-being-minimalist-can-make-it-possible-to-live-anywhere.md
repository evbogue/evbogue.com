<p><a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/bogue/4422750581/sizes/m/"><img class="alignleft" src="/assets/posts/how-being-minimalist-can-make-it-possible-to-live-anywhere-img1.jpg" alt="" width="263" height="350"/></a>Some people are content to live their lives in three places 95% of the time.</p>
<p>They valiantly wake up every morning, put on clothes, and walk the five steps to their car.</p>
<p>They fire it up and drive to work, where they spend twelve hours checking Facebook and doing what they&#8217;re told.</p>
<p>Then they go home, exhausted.</p>
<p>Once a year they hop aboard a flight to a cheap beach somewhere and spend a few days getting sunburned and sipping tequila.</p>
<p><strong>Does that life sound familiar?</strong></p>
<p>These people have convinced themselves that this is the only reality. There is no other option but to maintain the status-quo and deal.</p>
<p>Until they get to retire at some point impossibly far into the future, after they&#8217;ve wasted their youth. Then what?</p>
<p>Well you might be taking that path, but it&#8217;s not the only option.</p>
<p>There are people who&#8217;ve decided to opt out of this life sentence of working until you die.</p>
<p><strong>These people want to teach you how to free yourself (if you want to.)</strong></p>
<p>I want to introduce you to my friend Karol Gajda.</p>
<p>Karol went from having the house and an expensive car to living and working from anywhere in very little time.</p>
<p>Currently he&#8217;s teaching people how to attain his level of freedom over at <a href="http://www.ridiculouslyextraordinary.com/">Ridiculously Extraordinary</a>. He just made a guitar with his bare hands in India! How awesome is that?</p>
<p>You can do this too, Karol can teach you how.</p>
<p>A few weeks ago Karol emailed me a <a href="http://www.farbeyondthestars.com/minimalistquickstartguide.pdf">Minimalist Quick Start Guide</a> based off my work in <a href="http://www.artofbeingminimalist.com/"><em>The Art of Being Minimalist</em></a>.</p>
<p>I&#8217;ve delayed too long in releasing it,  so I&#8217;m just going to put it up here now!</p>
<p>The Minimalist Quick Start Guide explains Karol&#8217;s own journey towards living a minimalist life free from the confines of society&#8217;s expectation. It&#8217;s awesome, it&#8217;s free.</p>
