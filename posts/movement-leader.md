*"A Leader is a Dealer in Hope." -- Napoleon Bonaparte*

A movement is created by a leader.

This individual is the one that commands the idea and leads a small group of people to change.

He focuses attention, speaks truth when it is needed, solves problems when they arise, and makes the difficult choices that appear in the creation of any movement.

Leaders are born in extraordinary situations. When they look back at their lives, they start to see a common thread: that they have always done this work, and they always will.

A leader has to be willing to be seen as being wrong to a large group of people (the status-quo) in order to risk being right for a small group of people who are willing to change (those who are remarkable.)

This leader is you.
