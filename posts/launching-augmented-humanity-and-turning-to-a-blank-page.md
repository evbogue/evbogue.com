<p>The density of a moment contains information stretching back to the beginning of the universe.</p>
<p>Looking deeply into someone&#8217;s eyes can transfer more information than you&#8217;ll ever receive online.</p>
<p>On February 15th, 2011, a few things are going to happen:</p>
<p>1. <em>Augmented Humanity</em> is launching exclusively on <a href="http://www.ebookling.com/">Ebookling</a>. It&#8217;s my new e-book, about second selves and mental cybernetics. The price will be $30.</p>
<p>2. <a href="http://www.ebookling.com/">Ebookling</a> is re-launching as an beautifully simple space to purchase e-books worth reading. Colin and Miles are working decisively to craft an incredible platform.</p>
<p>The Ebookling launch is pretty self-explanatory, so I&#8217;ll let it happen when it happens.</p>
<p>If you buy the book you&#8217;ll get a link you can retweet, email, or link to which will give you 25% commission on any book sales from Ebookling. No one has ever created an e-bookstore that pays people to read in this way.</p>
<p>3. I&#8217;m going to wipe <a href="http://www.farbeyondthestars.com/">Far Beyond The Stars</a> completely, cleaning the slate. All of the writing and research is being condensed into the two e-books. <em>Minimalist Business</em> will be relaunching exclusively on Ebookling on the 15th, as well.</p>
<p>The archive of Far Beyond The Stars is back in the sidebar. I&#8217;m uncopywriting all of the work I&#8217;ve done on FBTS over the last year and a half (except the e-books). You have until the morning of February 15th to do whatever you want with the content. Archive it, republish it, read your favorite article one last time.</p>
<p></p>
<p>The future of the work is in your hands.</p>
<p>Keep an eye on <a href="http://www.gwenbell.com/">Gwen</a> too, she&#8217;s up to some mischief as well.</p>
<p>Anyone who <a href="http://www.letter.ly/evbogue">signs up for the Letter.ly</a> ($25 per month) will receive a free copy of <em>Augmented Humanity</em> on February 15th. I will continue to write about the deeper work that I&#8217;m doing there &#8212; work that won&#8217;t be available anywhere else.</p>
<p>I&#8217;ll be deleting the feedburner list, so you&#8217;ll have to resubscribe to the new blog (when/where it launches) or follow me on Twitter.
