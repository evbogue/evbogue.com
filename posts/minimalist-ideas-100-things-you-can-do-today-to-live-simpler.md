<p>When people think about minimalism, some of them think that it&#8217;s really hard and it involves throwing out all of their stuff and being a freegan or something. It doesn&#8217;t. Minimalism is about small steps toward a simple goal. It&#8217;s totally achievable, but the idea of running around with just a backpack is really terrifying for folks who have a house full of stuff and a packed schedule.</p>
<p><strong>You&#8217;re not going to be a minimalist overnight, but by taking tiny steps you&#8217;re getting there slowly, and more important, simply.</strong></p>
<p>If you can do one of the things on this list today, you&#8217;ll be a little more minimalist, and that helps! Yes, they&#8217;re small, but that&#8217;s fine. Maybe try doing one of these once a day for a hundred days? Maybe some of them will stick, and then you&#8217;re doing 100 things that make you more of a minimalist. That&#8217;d be pretty cool, and I think you can do it!</p>
