## What we cover

**Primary beat:** Silicon Valley — founders, VCs and their LPs, AI labs, Big Tech labor, the libertarian-to-reactionary political pipeline, the hagiographic profiles, the launches that don't survive contact with reality.

**Secondary beat:** the media business, especially where it intersects with tech: who got hired, who got fired, who got paid, who got captured, who got sued.

We do not cover personal lifestyle, productivity, wellness, or hot takes about AI that any LLM could write.
