The single most important struggle of the artist in 2011 is **staying alive in the minds of the people of the net**.

There is no yesterday on the Net. There's only today. Knowing this, I don't aim to produce one masterpiece every month or every year.

Instead I...

1. Sit down
2. Do the work
3. Press publish

...[every single day](/posts/write-daily).

Doing the work is the best way I've found to stay alive in the minds of the people of the Net.

If I have some big $$ days, luck, or timeless masterpieces doing the work? Bonus.
