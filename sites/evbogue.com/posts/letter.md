Alright, so you've been reading Ev Bogue for awhile now. You get the blog posts, and you've watched him on Google+. He seems a little mysterious, even secretive sometimes. Why? What's he hiding? Nothing, probably.

Actually quite a lot. You see The Internet is a no-space. No-spaces are like airports: you have to act a certain way in them or someone comes along, carts you off, and strip searches you.

There was a time when I wrote everything that came to the top of my head on the Internet, and that time has eclipses. I write some things for the public web -- this is the surface. I write other things for our private web -- this is the depth.

What do we talk about on the Letter? Evolving Your Digital Work. Success, money, fame, power, and the future of the Internet. It all depends on what's alive in me today.

I hope you'll give it a chance for a month. It's less than a cup of coffee per day, and helps support all of the free work that I do online that you're already familiar with.

Best,
Ev Bogue, August 2011.
