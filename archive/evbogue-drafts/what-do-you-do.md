<p class="date">October 31, 2011</p>
<p itemprop="description"><p>I get this question a lot. Especially since I’ve started wandering again down through San Francisco to Puerto Vallarta. <strong>“What do you do?”</strong> is on the minds of many. </p>
<p>I’m surprised at how many people seem confused when I tell them what I do. I’m writing this post to clear things up.</p>
<p><strong>This is what I do:</strong></p>
<p>I’m a professional writer. </p>
<p>I write <a href="http://eepurl.com/eKhvg">free daily letters</a> as I move through the world. These letters gifts, but also invitations to go deeper at any time. I’m committed to writing free daily letters every single day. The most important element of being a writer, for me, is showing up to hit the publish button. These free daily letters show up every morning in your inbox. They’re the hors d’oeuvre.</p>
<p>I write <a href="/posts/evolve">Evolving Your Digital Work</a>. A growing number of committed readers support my on-going deeper writing here. I write letters twice a week. There’s no archive here. It’s about what I can bring you from where I am now. This is the main course.</p>
<p>I’m writing <a href="/posts/get-paid-to-write">Get Paid to Write</a> and <a href="/posts/taking-your-book-to-the-web">Taking Your Book to the Web</a>. Both are filled and in session right now. They’re wrapping up in November. After that? If you want to be notified about new or recurring products or services, sign up for <a href="http://eepurl.com/eKhvg">free daily letters</a> and check ‘sales/discounts’ to get a price-break on anything new.</p>
<p>It’s a delicate balance between charging for my work as a professional writer, and giving my work away to new/potential readers. I walk this edge daily.</p>
<p>I’m always curious: <a href="https://evbogue.wufoo.com/forms/what-do-you-want-to-know/">what do you want to know?</a></p>
<p>I prompt <a href="https://plus.google.com/116298207273891385223/posts">gatherings on Google+</a>.</p>
