<p itemprop="description"><p>Over the month of August, I decided to experiment. What if I write every single day for an entire month?</p>
<p>I’ve never published every day before. It was challenging to keep up. On some days, I felt tired, I wanted to skip, but I didn’t. Instead, I came up with something to write. Sometimes the work landed, other times it didn’t.</p>
<p>Publishing every day challenged me. It made me look outside the ideas that I’d been dwelling on. It helped me investigate new territory. Writing every day helped me see that I needed to untether from my past work, in order to evolve into the future.</p>
<p>It also made me ask some questions of my work. Does everyone want to hear from me every day? I wasn’t sure. So, I decided to give you options — now anyone can <a href="http://eepurl.com/eKhvg">sign up to receive blog posts either daily or weekly</a>. Do you want <a href="http://eepurl.com/eKhvg">access to deals or sales</a>, before I launch my products to the blog? Also an option. </p>
<p>Now that we’re entering September, I have questions in my mind, for you.</p>
<p>I’m not asking because I want to change myself to fit the whims of 26,000 readers. I can’t possibly make everyone happy. I also can’t write about anything I haven’t experienced myself. </p>
<p>I’d simply like to open the comments for you. I want to listen. </p>
<p>When do you benefit most from my writing? </p>
<p>Which of my posts/topics resonate best with you? </p>
<p>What do you want to know?</p>
<p>I’ll be listening in the comments.</p>
