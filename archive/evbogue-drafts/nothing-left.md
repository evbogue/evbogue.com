<p itemprop="description"><p><strong>Q: What happens when you untether from untethering?<br/>
</strong><br/>
I imagine there’s a space that I may come to exist in, far away from where I am now, when there won’t be anything I want to untether from. </p>
<p>I imagine it’s an empty space, somewhere just beyond uncertainty. It’s a space where I’m not running from anything, where I’m not gripping anything, where I’m not padding against what might happen. </p>
<p>Is this the space where I get to choose exactly what I want to do with every moment?</p>
