<p class="date">October 17, 2011</p>
<p itemprop="description"><p>I’m getting on train headed south from Seattle today. I’m not sure where I’m going, but I’ll let you know when I get there. I’ll be writing more about what’s up in my world on <a href="/posts/evolve">Evolving Your Digital Work</a>.</p>
<p>One of the most popular posts I wrote here on Evbogue.com last month was when I wrote about my <a href="/posts/personal-things">personal Things</a>. [Actually, the most popular posts I've ever written ever over the past two years of living out of a backpack have been about what I pack. I shouldn't be so surprised.]</p>
<p>I untethered from a whole bunch of stuff since the last post on my Things, and made some significant upgrades to my wardrobe. </p>
<p><strong>I’ve been living out of a bag for over two years now.</strong> Over time, I’ve streamlined what I need significantly. I used to pack three times as much as this when I didn’t know where I was going.</p>
<p>I don’t have a secret storage locker in the world somewhere with junk in it. These are all of my Things.</p>
