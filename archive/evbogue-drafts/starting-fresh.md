<p>Every month, I start fresh with a new <a href="/posts/how-i-write">Scrivener</a> file. It looks like this photo (above).</p>
<p>This way, I’m unburdened by the work I did last month. I’m not checking back in to see what I did. If I can’t remember what I did, what’s to remember?</p>
<p>There are only new words to write now.</p>
