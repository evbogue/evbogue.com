<p>One of the most common questions I get is simple:</p>
<p>What the frak is a cyborg?</p>
<p>The answer is less simple. The the modern day cybernetic human is explained thoroughly in <em><a href="http://ebookling.com/items/augmented-humanity-second-selves-mental-cybernetics-and-the-future-of-you?ref=TCUJSWPo">Augmented Humanity</a></em>. The feeling of actually being a cyborg is something you can only feel once you become one. Some will never feel this way, others were born this way &#8212; especially children who, as Eric Schmidt recently <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-IGfBGHDHRc">explained at DLD2011</a>, only have two settlings now: asleep and online.</p>
<p>One of the best ways to understand is to watch Amber Case&#8217;s TED talk <a href="http://www.ted.com/talks/lang/eng/amber_case_we_are_all_cyborgs_now.html">We&#8217;re All Cyborgs Now</a>. Amber and I met up in Boulder, CO., where we discussed cyborgs in depth. Her research into mental cybernetics and second selves deeply influenced the writing in my book. Please watch the video below:</p>
