<p itemprop="description"><p>The single most important struggle of the artist in 2011 is <strong>staying alive in the minds of the people of the net</strong>.</p>
<p>There is no yesterday on the Net. There’s only today. Knowing this, I don’t aim to product one masterpiece every month or every year.</p>
<p>Instead I…</p>
<ol>
<li>Sit down</li>
<li>Do the work</li>
<li>Press publish</li>
</ol>
<div>…<a href="/posts/write-daily">every single day</a>.</div>
<p>Doing the work is the best way I’ve found to stay alive in the minds of the people of the Net.</p>
<p>If I have some big $$ days, luck, or timeless masterpieces doing the work? Bonus.</p>
</p>
