<p><i>Written by Ev Bogue | Follow me on <a href="https://plus.google.com/116298207273891385223/">Google+</a></i></p>
<p>Taking Your Book the Web sold out last night, moments before 7pm. </p>
<p>It’s always strange to me how launches happen this way. I set a goal: 150 people. I put a deadline on the price: Tuesday at 7pm PST. And then, I hit it. </p>
<p>Last year, I read a book called <em><a href="http://www.amazon.com/Think-Grow-Rich-Napoleon-Hill/dp/0449214923">Think and Grow Rich</a></em> that outlines how “magic” like this happens. It sounds all woo-woo, until I actually do it a couple of times.</p>
<p>To be fair, one of the spaces went to a colleague pro-bono, and another went towards honoring an old commitment. Putting the project at 151 signups. </p>
<p>If you signed up, welcome to the project (you should have received your first email last night.)</p>
<p>If you haven’t, no worries! </p>
