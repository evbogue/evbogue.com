<p class="date">November 11, 2011</p>
<p itemprop="description"><p>As the year begins to end, I’m looking at which technologies have been rendered obsolete since the year began.</p>
<p>I remember the day I upgraded from Writeroom to Scrivener for my writing workflow. That upgraded experience changed many things for me.</p>
<p>I also remember the day I deleted my Twitter. It was June 16th. </p>
<p>We’ve also seen some upgrades this year. A few days ago Gmail got a giant upgrade. My experience so far has been positive.</p>
<p>Here’s my experiment for you:</p>
<p>Write down all of the hardware and software technologies that you use on a daily basis (that immediately come to mind.) These can be desktop Apps, cloud Apps or something else entirely.</p>
<p>Is there anything on the list that you can upgrade?</p>
<p>Is there anything on the list that you can untether from?</p>
<p>//</p>
<p>Let me know what you’re upgrading or untethering from, <a href="mailto:evbogue@gmail.com">reply to me</a>! I want to hear about your experience.</p>
