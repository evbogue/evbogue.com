<p itemprop="description"><p>I’ve already forgotten what the old version of Gmail was like. </p>
<p>I’ve been updated for three days, and I don’t remember never having faces to the left of all my conversations. Then I get an email from someone without a Face, it seems strange and alien. I wonder where their face is when I get an email without one. </p>
<p>It seems strange to me, 3 days after Faces started to become a part of my daily emailing.</p>
<p>The Internet has changed a lot in the last year. Every experiment that I did last year? I automatically assume that the results are void. The only experiments that I trust are the ones that I’m doing now.</p>
<p>3 days ago, it was okay to not have my face show up in your email. Today? It seems weird.</p>
<p>Experiment with getting your face next to your email address in Gmail. It’s a simple change, one that changes everything.</p>
