<div class="entry">
<p class="date">October 12, 2011</p>
<p itemprop="description"><p>These are the two most important tools that I use to explore the digital world.</p>
<ol>
<li>I experiment.</li>
<li>I measure.</li>
</ol>
<p>Experiments are daring, they’re inspiring, they’re risky. I don’t talk about experiments before I do them, that would be <a href="/posts/hypothetical">hype</a>. Experiments can be small changes for 30 days or they can be a single action.</p>
<p>Measurements are realistic. They ask me this: did that work?</p>
<p>There’s no sense in continuing to run an experiment when it isn’t working.</p>
<p>If an experiment isn’t working, <a href="http://evbogue.com/category/untether/">I untether</a>.</p>
</p>
