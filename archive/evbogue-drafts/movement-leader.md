<p>A movement is created by a leader. </p>
<p>This individual is the one that commands the idea and leads a small group of people to change. </p>
<p>He focuses attention, speaks truth when it is needed, solves problems when they arise, and makes the difficult choices that appear in the creation of any movement.</p>
<p>Leaders are born in extraordinary situations. When they look back at their lives, they start to see a common thread: that they have always done this work, and they always will.</p>
<p>A leader has to be willing to seen as being wrong to a large group of people (the status-quo) in order to risk being right for a small group of people who are willing to change (those who are remarkable.)</p>
<p>This leader is you.</p>
