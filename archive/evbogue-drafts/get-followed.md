<p itemprop="description"><p>I imagine <a href="https://plus.google.com/116298207273891385223/posts">Google+</a> gets kind of lonely if you aren’t being followed by anyone. So here’s three strategies to employ.</p>
<ol>
<li>Follow 100 people. Keep this circle private. Rotate these people out depending on who you care about. Keep it around 100, no more, no less.</li>
<li>Follow less popular people. The top 1% get the most +1s, shares, etc. Ignore them.</li>
<li>Listen to the people to whom no one is listening. Ask these people questions. You’ll find out what they need. Give it to them.</li>
</ol>
