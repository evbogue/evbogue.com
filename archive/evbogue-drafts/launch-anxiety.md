<p itemprop="description"><p>I get scared just before I’m about to launch a Thing.</p>
<p>I find a million reasons that I’m not going to do it. </p>
<p>For example: no one needs this. I’m not capable of teaching this. I can’t tell this from experience. No one will sign up. No one needs this. I can’t, I can’t, I can’t. </p>
<p>Then I push through the initial fear and ship. People buy it. I do the work. Then, the cycle starts again.</p>
<p>The hard part is, launching never gets any easier. It’s always going to bring up doubts, anxiety, and fear. </p>
<p>I ship anyway.</p>
