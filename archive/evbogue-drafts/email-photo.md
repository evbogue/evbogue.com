<p itemprop="description"><p><a href="http://evbogue.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/L1020408.jpg"><img src="http://evbogue.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/L1020408.jpg" alt="" title="L1020408" width="50%" class="alignright size-full wp-image-964"/></a>Recently, faces started to show up in my Gmail inbox.</p>
<p>I like this, because I can feel a real human connection with the human on the other end.</p>
<p>I’m starting to become conscious that my face is showing up in other’s email. I believe this is happening because I keep a <a href="https://plus.google.com/116298207273891385223/">Google profile</a>. </p>
<p>Another way to keep my face showing up consistently across the web is by maintaining a <a href="http://en.gravatar.com/">Gravatar</a>.</p>
<p>Have you thought about how your face is showing up in other’s inboxes?</p>
<p>How does this change email for you? </p>
