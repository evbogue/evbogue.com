<h4>What is a digital sabbatical?</h4>
<p>A digital sabbatical is a break from the Internet.</p>
<p>It’s a retreat from the constant back and forth of digital connection.</p>
<p>It’s a chance to look within myself for answers.</p>
<p><strong>I won’t be:</strong></p>
<p>Checking email</p>
<p>Tweeting</p>
<p>Searching</p>
<p>Instagr.aming</p>
<p>Writing for the public web</p>
<p><strong>I will be:</strong></p>
<p>Writing <a href="http://www.letterly.net/evbogue">The Letter</a></p>
<p>Exploring my world</p>
<p>Enjoying the sunshine, swimming in warm lakes, practicing yoga on rocks, and drinking wine as the sun sets.</p>
<h4>Why take a digital sabbatical?</h4>
<p>In our hyper-connected society, it’s more important than ever to take the time to pull the plug once in awhile. The Internet can turn from a tool into an addiction. Many people don’t even know that they’re addicted in the first place.</p>
<p>This is why I choose to take a break, to reconvene with myself, to craft my work from a non-connected place.</p>
