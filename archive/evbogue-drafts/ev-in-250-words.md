<p>Ev Bogue is a cybernetic yogi supporting mindfulness at the edge of human evolution. He&#8217;s the author of <a href="http://ebookling.com/items/augmented-humanity-second-selves-mental-cybernetics-and-the-future-of-you?ref=TCUJSWPo">Augmented Humanity</a> and <a href="http://ebookling.com/items/minimalist-business-how-to-live-and-work-anywhere?ref=TCUJSWPo">Minimalist Business</a>.</p>
<p>Ev is speaking at <a href="http://www.thisismindful.com/">This is Mindful</a> in Melbourne Australia in May 2011. He has spoken about simple businesses at Stanford University. His work with minimalism and businesses has been featured on the CBS Evening News, The Guardian UK, and The Huffington Post. He&#8217;s a 200 hour certified yoga teacher, studying at Yoga to the People in San Francisco.</p>
<p>Ev has worked at <a href="http://www.nymag.com/">New York Magazine</a> and <a href="http://www.gawker.com/">Gawker Media</a>. In late 2009, he left New York to live and work from anywhere, creating a simple online business with zero over-head (details on this in <em><a href="http://www.minimalistbusiness.com/">Minimalist Business</a></em>). He&#8217;s lived in New York, Chicago, Portland, San Francisco, Oakland, and Boulder. Before discovering minimalism was a side-effect of embracing The Cloud he published <em>The Art of Being Minimalist</em> (out of print).</p>
<p>Ev grew up in Chicago, and dropped out of school at a very young age to play with technology full time. He coded his first website on GeoCities when he was 12 years old, where he let people download highly evolved simulated fish. Eventually he began dancing in order to balance out all of the geek. He graduated from NYU with a degree in Dance and Journalism in 2003.</p>
<p>In November 2010, Ev discovered that he&#8217;d created a second self that took care of him, and was quickly evolving advanced skills in mental cybernetics &#8211;which he wrote about in <a href="http://ebookling.com/items/augmented-humanity-second-selves-mental-cybernetics-and-the-future-of-you?ref=TCUJSWPo">Augmented Humanity</a>.</p>
