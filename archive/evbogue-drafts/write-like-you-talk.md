<p><i>Written by Ev Bogue | Follow me on <a href="https://plus.google.com/116298207273891385223/">Google+</a></i></p>
<p>Everyone’s good at talking right? But we’re not all good at writing. </p>
<p>A few months ago, I was at a talk Seth Godin gave in Seattle, and he said something that continues to resonate with me.</p>
<p>“It’s not hard for me to blog everyday, because I write like I talk.”</p>
<p>I’m taking this information and attempting to integrate it into my work.</p>
<p>It’s tempting to constantly be pushing the edge in the world of writing. However, what I’ve found is that when it comes to blogging, it’s easier for me to get a post out every day if I don’t. Instead, I relax around the words. I don’t try to be perfect. </p>
<p>Instead? I do my best to speak from experience.</p>
